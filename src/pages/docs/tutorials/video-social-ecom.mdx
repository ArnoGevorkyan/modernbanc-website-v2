### How to use Modernbanc to enable Video Social E-commerce

Steps:

1. Connect bank account entities for streamers and the platform
2. Connect user accounts and their preferred payment method
3. Create a workflow
4. Link trigger to the workflow when a product is purchased by the user
5. create steps to handle the payment flow from user, to a holding account, distribute to streamers, and take the platform cut
